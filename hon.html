Hon. Godwin Offiono — serving Ogoja/Yala

Dedicated to community development, security, education and youth empowerment. Below are some of the contributions and initiatives he has supported.

Constituency Representation

Represents the people of Ogoja/Yala Federal Constituency in the House of Representatives and is an active member of the People's Democratic Party (PDP).

Security & Police Support

Facilitated construction of a divisional police station and officers' quarters in Okpoma, Yala LGA to boost local security and response capacity.

Rural Electrification & Lighting

Installed solar-powered street lights across strategic locations in Ogoja and Yala to improve safety and night-time activity.

Agricultural Support

Distributed fertilizers, hybrid water-pumping machines and farming inputs to boost productivity among local farmers.

Education & Youth

Provided educational materials to schools across the constituency and founded youth initiatives including a football club to nurture talent.

Humanitarian & Welfare

Delivered welfare support including food distributions, cash grants and assistance to vulnerable community members through the G & S Offiono Foundation.
